# Bug ID: Bug-030
 
# Title:
###  The take payment button blocks the cancell button of a placed order.
 
# Module:
###  UI/Menu
 
# Severity:
###  high
 
# priority: 
###  high
 
# Environment
###  android
 
# steps to reproduce:
 ### login to the mobile application
 ### click on the cart menu
 ### select any meal
 ### click on take payment
 ### click on pay later
 ### scroll down to the order items section of the order
 
# Actual Result
 ### The cancell order button is block by the take payment button
 ### This makes it hard to press cancell without pressing take payment
 
# Expected Result:
 ### The take payment button must be well positioned
 ### This makes it possible for the cancell button to be easily pressed
